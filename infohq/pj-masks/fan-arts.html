Fan Arts

A PJ Masks fan arts is an artwork based on the PJ Masks TV series that is created by fans.

PJ Masks Save The Day Admin knows that fan arts related to the PJ Masks by the PJ Masks fans existed so we have created a new section in our site specially for this. A place for everyone to upload or read fan arts by PJ Masks fans.

If you have a completed fan arts, you could submit your fan arts to us so it could be uploaded into PJ Masks Save The Day website. We will review each and ALL fan arts in a case-by-case basis to ensure it follow our community guidelines. You could upload via the Google Form here.
