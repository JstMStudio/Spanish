OC

OC means an original character. PJ Masks fan might create their own OC for their roleplay and fan fiction story.

PJ Masks Save The Day Admin Information knows that OC related to the PJ Masks by the PJ Masks fans existed so we have created a new section in our site specially for this. A place for everyone to upload or read OC by PJ Masks fans.

If you have a completed OC, you could submit your OC to us so it could be uploaded into PJ Masks Save The Day website. We will review each and ALL OC in a case-by-case basis to ensure it follow our community guidelines. You could upload via the Google Form here.
