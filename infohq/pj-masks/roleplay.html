Roleplay

Role-playing or roleplaying is the changing of one's behaviour to assume a role, either unconsciously to fill a social role, or consciously to act out an adopted role.

PJ Masks Save The Day Admin knows that roleplay related to the PJ Masks by the PJ Masks fans existed so we have created a new section in our site specially for this. A place for everyone to upload or read roleplay by PJ Masks fans.

If you have a completed roleplay, you could submit your roleplay to us so it could be uploaded into PJ Masks Save The Day website. We will review each and ALL roleplay in a case-by-case basis to ensure it follow our community guidelines. You could upload via the Google Form here.
